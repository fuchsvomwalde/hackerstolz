About

We want to build a platform for students (of all areas) and young professionals to prototype awesome ideas...

...by bringing companies, students, IT-experts and young professionals together.

This time it's all about Education, so build apps that help learning, analyse big data of the education sector, build quizzes for everyone etc.

Meet new friends, partners or future employees.

Organized by the association Hackerstolz in cooperation with the Mannheim Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship and the SquareRoots Mannheim
