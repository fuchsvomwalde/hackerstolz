About

We want to build a platform for students (from all disciplines) and young professionals to prototype awesome ideas.

This time it's all about education, so build apps that help learning, analyze big data from the education sector, build quizzes for everyone etc.

Meet new friends, partners or future employees.

Organized by the association Hackerstolz in cooperation with the Mannheim Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship and the SquareRoots Mannheim

FAQ

Why education as the guiding theme?

Education is a major challenge in today's society. It is so essential because...

there is an ever increasing amount of things to learn

there are more opportunities to learn than ever before

it becomes harder to decide what is worth learning

What to expect?

During the EduHack Mannheim, teams will come together to revise what and how society learns in a creative and open-minded environment. The results will be prototypes that outline their ideas. These could be, but are not limited to projects that...

process and visualize data to facilitate learning about a certain topic

tools that give recommendations about what to learn

tools that track and streamline the learning progress

analyses about the current state of the education sector based on public data sets

Why attend?

1. Learn new skills and apply the latest technology.

2. Build your network by collaborating with innovators and experts across multiple disciplines.

3. Build a technological prototype as a solution to real world challenges in education.

4. Join a community of people committed to igniting innovation in digital education.

5. Win awesome prices and magnify your reputation by sharing your ideas.

6. Meet new people & have lots of fun!

Who is EduHacks for?

Everyone is welcome to join the event. We strive for an interdisciplinary mixture of participants, where everybody can contribute their specific skills. However, we recommend having a background in one of the following areas:

Programming

Web Design

Statistics & Data Analysis

Communication Design

Educating or teaching
